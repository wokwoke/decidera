# Purpose

This document defines the long-term vision of Decidera.

It describes what Decidera ultimately seeks to become, independent of any specific technology, programming language, or implementation.

The vision serves as the guiding direction for every architectural decision throughout the project's lifetime.

---

# Vision Statement

Decidera aims to become an open, evolvable Decision-to-Artifact framework that enables creators to transform structured discussions into reliable, traceable, and executable project artifacts.

Rather than replacing human thinking, Decidera exists to strengthen it.

Its purpose is to help creators organize ideas, preserve knowledge, document decisions, and reduce cognitive overhead throughout the software development lifecycle.

---

# Why Decidera Exists

Modern software projects often focus on implementation while neglecting the reasoning behind it.

Important discussions disappear.

Architectural decisions become undocumented.

Knowledge becomes fragmented across chats, documents, repositories, and personal notes.

As projects evolve, the original intent is gradually lost.

Decidera exists to preserve that intent.

---

# Long-Term Vision

In the long term, Decidera seeks to become a complete design and decision framework capable of supporting projects from their earliest ideas through long-term evolution.

This includes:

- Structuring discussions.
- Preserving findings.
- Recording decisions.
- Managing architectural knowledge.
- Generating project artifacts.
- Supporting implementation workflows.
- Maintaining traceability throughout the project lifecycle.

---

# Human-Centered Design

Decidera is designed to augment human decision-making rather than replace it.

The framework assumes that:

- Humans define direction.
- Discussions generate understanding.
- Decisions create clarity.
- Documentation preserves knowledge.
- Automation accelerates execution.

Artificial Intelligence is considered a supporting capability rather than the foundation of the framework.

---

# Design Philosophy

Decidera prioritizes:

- Clarity over complexity.
- Architecture over implementation.
- Decisions over assumptions.
- Documentation over memory.
- Evolution over rigid planning.
- Transparency over hidden processes.

These principles guide every part of the framework.

---

# Success Criteria

The vision of Decidera will be considered successful if creators can:

- Maintain clear architectural knowledge over time.
- Trace every important decision.
- Reduce cognitive workload during development.
- Generate consistent project artifacts.
- Evolve projects without losing historical context.

---

# Relationship with OTONOM

Although Decidera originated during the design of OTONOM, it is intentionally designed as an independent framework.

OTONOM is one application of Decidera, not its definition.

The framework should remain useful for many different types of software projects.

---

# Looking Forward

The long-term vision extends beyond documentation.

As the framework matures, Decidera may incorporate capabilities such as intelligent assistants, provider integrations, workflow automation, and collaborative discussion models.

These capabilities should enhance the framework without changing its core philosophy.

---

# Summary

Decidera is not a tool for writing code.

It is a framework for making better engineering decisions.

By transforming discussions into structured decisions and executable artifacts, Decidera aims to make software development more transparent, maintainable, and sustainable.
