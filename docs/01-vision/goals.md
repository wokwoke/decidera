# Purpose

This document defines the strategic goals of Decidera.

The goals translate the project's vision and mission into long-term objectives that guide architectural decisions and future development.

Unlike implementation milestones, these goals describe what Decidera ultimately aims to accomplish as a framework.

---

# Primary Goal

The primary goal of Decidera is to establish a reliable Decision-to-Artifact framework that enables creators to build software through structured discussions, documented decisions, and traceable artifacts.

---

# Strategic Goals

## 1. Reduce Cognitive Load

Enable creators to focus on design and decision-making rather than managing fragmented information across multiple tools.

---

## 2. Preserve Knowledge

Capture discussions, findings, and architectural decisions as long-lived project assets rather than temporary conversations.

---

## 3. Improve Traceability

Ensure that every significant artifact can be traced back to the discussions and decisions that produced it.

---

## 4. Encourage Better Architecture

Promote architecture-first and documentation-first practices before implementation begins.

---

## 5. Standardize Engineering Processes

Provide a consistent workflow for transforming discussions into executable project artifacts.

---

## 6. Support Long-Term Evolution

Allow projects to evolve without losing historical context or architectural intent.

---

## 7. Enable Automation

Prepare a structured foundation where repetitive engineering tasks can be automated without sacrificing transparency or quality.

---

## 8. Remain Technology Independent

Design the framework around concepts and processes rather than specific programming languages, AI models, or software platforms.

---

# Success Indicators

Decidera moves closer to its goals when creators are able to:

- Understand project history through documentation.
- Reconstruct architectural decisions.
- Produce consistent project artifacts.
- Reduce unnecessary redesign.
- Maintain projects over long periods without losing context.
- Adopt the framework across different types of software projects.

---

# Continuous Improvement

The goals described in this document are expected to remain stable.

However, new goals may emerge as the framework evolves and new architectural challenges are discovered.

Any significant change should be documented through the project's decision process.

---

# Summary

The goals of Decidera define the long-term outcomes the framework seeks to achieve.

They provide measurable direction while remaining independent of specific implementations or technologies.
